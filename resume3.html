EDUCATION / TRAINING

Bennington College,  Bennington, Vt.

B.A. with a concentration in dance and mathematics. Developed and strengthened a personal movement vocabulary and choreographic voice through a variety of composition, improvisation, and technique courses; choreographed and performed solo and ensemble work. Faculty included: Dana Reitz, Susan Sgorbati, Terry Creach, Daniel Roberts, Soleymane Badolo, Lindsey Dietz Marchant, Joseph Poulson, Richard Siegal.

RELATED WORK EXPERIENCE

The Forsythe Company,   Frankfurt, Germany

Apprentice; attended company class, observed and participated in the rehearsal process, traveled with the company on tour; aided in the ALTANA Cultural Foundation's education program providing middle school students with access to dance and the arts.

Student Endowment for the Arts,  Bennington, Vt.

Member of an all-student board devoted to fnancially supporting and educating the artists of Bennington College while strengthening the role of the arts within the community at large; reviewed grant proposals, allocated funds, and organized student showcases.

José Mateo Ballet Theatre,  Cambridge, Ma.

Helped with developmental work and institutional advancement; aided in writing solicitations and grant proposals; organized the frst annual "Talk About Dance" event: a discourse on harnessing the power of dance to create social change.

Green Street Studios,   Cambridge, Ma.

Assisted in the management of an open-class studio; compiled and organized several years of archival material; maintained the organization's online identities.

The Potter's Shop & School,  Needham, Ma.

Helped with the general maintenance of a pottery studio which housed a diverse community of atists; cleaned the studio, mixed glazes, loaded and unloaded kilns; kept up member relations.
