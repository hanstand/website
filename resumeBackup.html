EDUCATION / TRAINING

Bennington College  |  Bennington, Vt.  |  Fall 2009 - Spring 2013

Bachelor of Arts with a concentration in dance and mathematics

Developed and strengthened a personal movement vocabulary and choreographic voice through a variety of composition, improvisation, and technique courses; choreographed and performed solo and ensemble work. Faculty included: Dana Reitz, Susan Sgorbati, Terry Creach, Daniel Roberts, Soleymane Badolo, Lindsey Dietz Marchant, Joseph Poulson, Richard Siegal.
