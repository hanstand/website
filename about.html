Hannah Simmons | about

© Randy H. Goodman

Hannah Simmons is a Boston-based choreographer and performer. She recieved her B.A. in Dance and Mathematics from Bennington College, studying under Susan Sgorbati, Dana Reitz, and Terry Creach, among others.

Hannah has worked with José Mateo Ballet Theatre (Cambridge), The Forsythe Company (Frankfurt), and Green Street Studios (Cambridge). She has performed in orignal works by Adele Myers and Edisa Weeks at the Institute of Contemporary Arts in Boston as part of Summer Stages Dance. At Bennington, she served as a member of the Student Endowment for the Arts, an all-student board devoted to financially supporting and educating the artists of the college while strengthening the role of the arts within the community at large.

Her own work centers around the intersection of dance, math, and technology.
